Venues

Bluewater Private Island

A 1.1-hectare private island (65 meters away from the beachfront) is a unique place that offers breathtaking view of the open sea. It is perfect for romantic, intimate and exclusive beach wedding ceremonies and receptions. For grand celebrations and large events, our Bluewater private island can seat up to 1,300 guests.

Beachfront

A 110-meter white sand beach surrounded by golden coconut palms, talisay trees and thatch huts. This backdrop coupled with great food and captivating sounds of the sea make for an enjoyable tropical party and beach weddings.

Pecnic Grove

Located between our main swimming pool and the beach, it is one of the areas in the resort whose landscape remains unchanged since the resort opened its gates to the public. There remains ancient and stately Banyan trees providing a lush canopy that keeps the atmosphere a few centigrade cooler than the rest of the resort. As the seasons change, guests can also witness the color of the green canopies provided by the Banyans and other endemic tree species that dot the landscape change its shade, filtering sunlight in different hues that gives a certain feel of intimacy to your special events.

Convention Hall

Our Convention Hall is ideal for business conferences, seminars and meetings. It can also hold private functions and parties. The Convention Hall is located at the ground floor of our Convention Center.

Conference Rooms - Bargas | Balete

Located at the second floor of our Convention Center, Bluewater Maribago Beach Resort has two conference rooms named after two trees that can be found in the resort - Bagras (Conference Room 1) and Balete (Conference Room 2). Our conference rooms are perfect venues for intimate gatherings, corporate entertaining and cocktail parties.

Bluewater Gallery

The Bluewater Gallery makes an interesting venue for small gatherings and meetings with vibrant works of local artists serving as backdrop. It is centrally located with easy access to Allegro Restaurant and main pool.
